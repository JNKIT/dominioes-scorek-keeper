# Privacy Policy for Capicú

**Last Updated**: January 8, 2026
**Effective Date**: January 8, 2026

## Introduction

Capicú ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dominoes scorekeeper application developed by jnkit. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

Capicú is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- **No Analytics**: We do not collect usage data, analytics, or telemetry
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
- **No Location Data**: We do not access or collect device location
- **No Network Transmission**: The app does not send any data over the internet
- **No Third-Party Services**: We do not integrate analytics, advertising, or tracking services
- **No User Accounts**: The app does not require or support user accounts

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data ONLY on your device using local storage:

1. **Game Data**:
   - Game scores and rounds
   - Game timestamps and status
   - Team assignments
   - Capicú and Paso Redondo bonus events

2. **Player Information**:
   - Player names you create
   - Player statistics (games played, wins, losses)

3. **Team Information**:
   - Team names you create
   - Team member associations
   - Team statistics

4. **App Preferences**:
   - Language preference (English/Spanish)
   - Game configuration settings (target score, bonus points)

**This data is stored using SharedPreferences (Android's local key-value storage) and NEVER leaves your device.**

### Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties because:
- The app operates entirely offline
- No data leaves your device
- No backend servers or cloud services are used

### External Links

The app contains an optional link to our donation page (buymeacoffee.com/jnkit). When you tap this link:
- You are leaving the app and visiting an external website
- That website has its own privacy policy
- We do not track whether you visit the link

### Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since the app:
- Does not collect any personal information
- Operates entirely offline
- Does not transmit data

It is safe for users of all ages.

### Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device:
- Protected by your device's security features
- Not transmitted over networks
- Not stored on external servers
- Under your complete control

You can delete all app data at any time by:
1. Using Android's "Clear App Data" feature in Settings > Apps > Capicú
2. Uninstalling the app

### Open Source

The app's source code is available for review at https://github.com/joelsmith/dominoes_scorekeeper, confirming our privacy claims.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted:
- In the app (if we add update notifications)
- On our GitHub repository
- In the Google Play Store listing

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will reflect when changes were made.

### Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact:
- Email: contact@jnki.com

### Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data:
- There is no data to access, modify, or delete on any servers
- All your data is on your device under your control
- You can export your data using the app's backup feature
- You can delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling

### Compliance

This app complies with:
- Google Play Store policies
- GDPR (no personal data collected)
- COPPA (no data collection from children)
- CCPA (no personal information sold or shared)

## Summary

Capicú is a privacy-focused app that:
- ✅ Stores all data locally on your device
- ✅ Never transmits data over the internet
- ✅ Never collects personal information
- ✅ Contains no analytics, ads, or tracking
- ✅ Is fully functional offline
- ✅ Requires no special permissions

Your privacy is our priority.

Capicú está diseñado con la privacidad como principio fundamental:

La aplicación almacena los siguientes datos SOLO en tu dispositivo usando almacenamiento local:
